Ordering Rules

Sauce always on the sideDressings add 150–300 hidden calories. Use at most half of what they send.
Skip or sub the riceSaves 180–220 cal. Always swap for extra greens or vegetables.
Protein every single mealEvery meal targets 40–55g. Critical while injured — preserves muscle during deficit.
One rich item per meal maxAvocado, nuts, cheese, or heavy sauce — pick one per meal, not all of them.
2-egg breakfast (~140 cal)Leaves ~1,360 cal for lunch and dinner combined.
